A banana is an elongated, edible berry-like fruit from the large herbaceous flowering plant genus Musa. Commonly consumed as a dessert fruit (like the Cavendish variety), bananas are a rich source of fiber, potassium, vitamin B6, vitamin C, and various antioxidants and plant compounds. Bananas offer potential health benefits such as improved digestion and heart health, though they are a staple food in many tropical regions, with plantains (cooking bananas) also widely consumed
